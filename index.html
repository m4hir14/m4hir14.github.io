Ingredients:

For The Base

70g Self Rising Flour
12g Coconut Flour
150g Low Fat Greek Yogurt
5g Nutritional Yeast
75g Egg Whites

For The Sauce

120g Tomato Passata
1 tbsp dried basil
Pinch Of Garlic Granules

For The Topping

60g Grated Mozzarella
Handful Of Basil Leaves (optional)


High Protein Margherita Pizza

Serving Size: 1 Whole Pizza

Recipe Makes: 1 Serving

Macros: 630 calories, 71g carbs, 15g fat, 53g protein

Method:

1. Add your dry ingredients into your bowl and mix to avoid clumping. Then add your wet ingredients and mix till you get a thick paste consistency.

2. Lay a piece of parchment paper on a clean, flat surface. Optionally, lightly flour the area or use a silicone baking mat for ease.

3. Position your dough in the center of the parchment paper. If it is sticky, dust your hands or the dough lightly with flour.

4. Use your hands or a rolling pin to spread the dough. If using your hands, gently press and stretch the dough from the center outward in a circular motion. If using a rolling pin, roll from the center outwards, rotating the dough slightly to maintain an even shape.

5. As you spread the dough, focus on keeping the edges thicker than the center to form a nice, puffy crust. If the edges become too thin, push the dough back towards the center to even it out.

6. Once you reach the desired size (12-13 inches), make sure the dough's thickness is uniform. Adjust any uneven spots using your fingers or rolling pin.

7. . Now add the parchment with the crust on it to a pan and bake on 230 degrees C for 10 minutes or until crust is golden.

8. Carefully take the crust off the parchment paper and return it to the pan and spread your desired toppings evenly over the crust.

9. Place it back into the oven till the toppings are also golden.

10. Then slice up and enjoy!
